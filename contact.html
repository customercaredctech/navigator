Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to Your Queries

Have simple questions? Read through our FAQ list below to find immediate answers.

Does AdmissionNavigator charge any service fees for student matches?

No, building your profile and obtaining matching college recommendation lists on AdmissionNavigator is 100% free of charge. We are funded by our partner academic institutions.

Do you assist with student visa applications?

Yes, we have licensed immigration advisors (including Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultants - RCICs) on staff. Once you obtain an Letter of Acceptance (LOA) from your matched school, we walk you step-by-step through the student permit application process.

How long does the matching assessment take?

Our interactive student profile registration wizard contains exactly 10 questions and takes less than 5 minutes to complete. Based on your answers, matching options are immediately loaded on your dashboard.

What are the minimum GPA or English score requirements?

Requirements vary greatly by country and program. While top university master's degrees might require a 75%+ GPA and a 6.5 IELTS score, many excellent diploma programs have lower requirements. Our wizard checks all variations to find matching options.
