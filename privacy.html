Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

1. Introduction

AdmissionNavigator ("we", "us", or "our") operates the admissionnavigator.com portal. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of the personal data of our users, students, and recruitment partners. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and share personal information in compliance with the Canadian Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), general privacy guidelines, and other applicable global data regulation frameworks.

2. Information We Collect

To provide matching and application services, we collect various categories of information:

  • Profile Information: Name, contact details, email address, password, citizenship, and date of birth entered during registration.
  • Academic History: GPA scores, transcripts, highest academic degree completed, English language proficiency scores (IELTS/TOEFL).
  • Financial Credentials: Available yearly tuition budget bands and funding details to ensure matching with eligible college programs.
  • Device and Analytical Info: IP addresses, browsing metrics, and cookies to improve platform usability.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your personal information is used solely to coordinate student matches and university filings:

  • Processing your 10-Question Profile Wizard answers to calculate suitable university matches.
  • Submitting academic documents to your chosen partner institutions on your behalf.
  • Providing updates regarding visa filing, academic applications, and scholarship possibilities.
  • Preventing fraudulent activities and securing portal credentials.

4. Disclosure of Personal Information

We do NOT sell student data. We only share personal information with:

  • Partner academic institutions to evaluate admission options.
  • Authorized study permit advisers (RCIC consultants) assisting in student visa filings.
  • Third-party cloud storage and database suppliers operating under strict non-disclosure terms.

5. Security of Your Data

We apply robust industry standard security tools (including SSL encryptions, firewalls, and authorization checklists) to safeguard data against unauthorized alterations, leakage, or loss.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold, request corrections to errors, or request the deletion of your student account. To exercise these rights, please contact our privacy officer at privacy@admissionnavigator.com.
